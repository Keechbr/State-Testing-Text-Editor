Privacy Policy

Thank you for using the South Glens Falls Central School District Chrome Extension. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through our extension.

No Data Collection

We want to assure you that we do not collect any personal information or data from users through our Chrome Extension. We do not track your browsing activities, collect your IP address, or gather any other information that could identify you personally.

Information Usage

Since we do not collect any data from users, there is no information to use. The extension solely provides functionality relevant to the services of the South Glens Falls Central School District without any data collection.

Data Security

While we do not collect any user data, we are committed to ensuring the security of our extension. We employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of any potential data that may be processed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here. Your continued use of the South Glens Falls Central School District Chrome Extension after any changes indicate your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
